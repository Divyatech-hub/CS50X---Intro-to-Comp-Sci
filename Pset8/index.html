Project 1

There are millions around the world who suffer from correctable visual defects due to their impoverished conditions, according to statistics from the World Health Organization. They remain unable to afford spectacles to improve their sight. My project, “Gift of Sight”, aims at helping the needy by providing them with free, hygienic spectacle frames collected from peoples’ homes and thereby, giving them an opportunity to see better at an affordable rate.
